What it is

A calm little audit for your Instagram

A Confidence Session is a one-off review of your Instagram page, designed to help you understand what’s working, what isn’t, and what to focus on next.

It’s perfect if you’re not ready for full social media management yet, but you know your content needs a bit more structure, clarity and “right, okay, I know what I’m doing now” energy.

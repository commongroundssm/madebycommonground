What it is

Get grounded before you grow

Before you can post confidently, your brand needs a clear foundation. Groundwork helps you figure out the colours, fonts, visuals and overall feel of your business, so your online presence looks intentional instead of thrown together.

It’s not about making your brand look like everyone else’s. It’s about creating a direction that feels like you, makes sense to your audience, and gives you something solid to build from.

Think of it as your brand’s starting point: less guesswork, more clarity.

How it works

The process

This is designed to feel simple and helpful, not like you’ve been handed a giant branding textbook and told to fend for yourself.

1. Brand questionnaire

You’ll answer a few questions about your business, audience, style preferences and what currently feels unclear.

2. Visual direction

I’ll pull together your moodboard, colour direction, font suggestions and overall brand feel so your business has a clearer look.

3. Brand foundations

You’ll receive your brand direction, Canva guidance, templates and style notes so you can start showing up with more consistency.
